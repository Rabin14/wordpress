Tripura Police is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 1488 posts of Riflemen (General Duty\/ Tradesmen)<\/strong>. The vacancies are in Indian Reserve (IR) Battalions against Inside State quota and Outside State quota. The posts are Temporary but likely to be Permanent. Both Male and Female candidates are eligible to apply. Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF below) along with testimonials. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below \u2014 <\/span><\/p>\n
TRIPURA POLICE IS INVITING APPLICATION FOR 1488 POSTS OF RIFLEMEN (GENERAL DUTY\/ TRADESMEN). LAST DATE \u2014 08\/06\/2019<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n

1. RIFLEMEN (GENERAL DUTY)<\/strong>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 1350 Nos. (Inside State quota: 1013 posts (UR-365, SC-230, ST-418) & Outside State quota: 337 posts (UR))<\/em>
Educational Qualification:<\/strong> Matriculation or equivalent from a recognized Board.
Age Limit:<\/strong> Minimum 18 years and maximum 23 years as on 01\/07\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/strong> Rs.5700- 24000\/- with Grade Pay Rs.2100\/-<\/p>\n

2. RIFLEMEN (TRADESMEN)<\/strong>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 138 Nos. (Inside State quota: 105 posts (UR-39, SC-23, ST-43) & Outside State quota: 33 posts (UR))<\/em>
Trade wise vacancies:<\/u><\/b> <\/p>\n

i) Cook: 48 posts
ii) Water Carrier: 30 posts
iii) Barber: 14 posts
iv) Dhobi: 16 posts
v) Sweeper: 30 posts<\/div>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/strong> Matriculation or equivalent from a recognized Board and-
i) Two years work experience in respective trades.
OR
ii) One year certificate course from Industrial Training Institute\/ Vocational Institute with at least one year experience in the trade.
OR
iii) Two years Diploma from Industrial Training Institute in the trade or similar trade.
Relaxation:<\/b> SC & ST candidates should have passed at least Class VIII.
Age Limit:<\/strong> Minimum 18 years and maximum 23 years as on 01\/07\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/strong> Rs.5700- 24000\/- with Grade Pay Rs.2100\/-<\/p>\n

The vacancies are in Indian Reserve (IR) Battalions against Inside State quota and Outside State quota. The posts are Temporary but likely to be Permanent. Both Male and Female candidates are eligible to apply.<\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/strong>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST and 03 years for OBC category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any \u2014 As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

Physical Standards:<\/b>
(i) Male Candidates: Height<\/u> \u2014 170 cms (160 cms for ST candidates); Chest (unexpanded)<\/u> \u2014 80 cms (76 cms for ST); Chest (expanded)<\/u> \u2014 85 cms (81 cms for ST)
(ii) Female Candidates: Height<\/u> \u2014 157 cms (147.5 cms for ST candidates)
Cut-off in Physical Standards<\/u>:<\/b> For candidates belonging to certain categories\/ communities or of certain States\/Areas; the cut-off in such candidate\u2019s Height\/ Chest\u2019s measurement will be as per Govt. norms. Please see officially released advertisement for more details.<\/i>
Candidates must not have knock knee, flat foot, varicose vein or squint in eyes and should possess high colour vision. They must be in good mental and bodily health and free from any physical defect likely to interfere with the efficient performance of the duties.<\/p>\n

Candidates\u2019 Selection:<\/strong> Selection of candidates will be through Physical Standard Test (PST), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Written Test and Personal Interview. For details regarding recruitment venues please see the Official Advt.
Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Tripura Police \u2014 www.tripurapolice.nic.in<\/u><\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates\u2019 selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/strong> Candidates must pay Rs.100\/- (Rs.50\/- for SC\/ST\/BPL Card Holder candidates)<\/strong> as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Indian Postal Order (IPO) drawn in favour of DGP, Tripura, Agartala<\/strong>.<\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/strong> Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF file below)<\/em> along with attested copies of testimonials in support of Educational Qualification, Caste, Age, Experience certificate (if applicable) and any other relevant documents
The envelope containing the print-out\/enclosures should be superscribed on top with \u2014 \u201cAPPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ________\u201d<\/strong><\/p>\n

Last Date and Address:<\/strong> The duly filled Application form along with enclosures should reach at the following address \u2014 The Assistant Inspector General of Police (TSR), Police Headquarters, Tripura, Fire Brigade Chowmuhani, Akhaura Road, Agartala, West Tripura, Pin- 799 001<\/strong>, on or before 08\/06\/2019.<\/strong>
Applications should be sent by Registered Post<\/strong>. The Applications will be received from 09\/03\/2019.<\/strong><\/p>\n
