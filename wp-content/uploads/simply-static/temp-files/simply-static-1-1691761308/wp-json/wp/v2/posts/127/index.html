District Health & Family Welfare Samiti, North 24 Parganas is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 40 posts of Counsellor, Lab Technician, Technical Supervisor, Staff Nurse, Medical Officer, Care Coordinator, Block Accounts Manager (BAM) and Record Keeper<\/b>. The candidates will be appointed on Contractual basis under National Health Mission, Department of Health & Family Welfare, North 24 Parganas. Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF below) along with testimonials. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (ST)<\/i>

ii) Working knowledge of Computer.

Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 40 years as on 01\/01\/2019.

ii) One year post qualification experience in the testing of blood and or preparation of blood components (in a Licensed Blood Bank) in case of candidates having Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology\/ Diploma in Laboratory Techniques (DMLT\/ DLT).
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 40 years as on 01\/01\/2019.
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.17,220\/- per month.<\/p>\n
