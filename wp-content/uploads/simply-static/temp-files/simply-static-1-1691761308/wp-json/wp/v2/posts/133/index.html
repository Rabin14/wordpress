Union Bank of India (UBI) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 181 posts of Fire Officer, Economist, Security Officer, Integrated Treasury Officer, Credit Officer<\/b> and Forex Officer<\/b>. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Union Bank of India (UBI) (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below \u2014<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

UNION BANK OF INDIA (UBI) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 181 POSTS OF FIRE OFFICER, ECONOMIST, SECURITY OFFICER, INTEGRATED TREASURY OFFICER, CREDIT OFFICER AND FOREX OFFICER. LAST DATE \u2014 29.03.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. FIRE OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> B.E (Fire Engineering) from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board\/ Divisional Officers course in National Fire Service College, Nagpur.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 10 years as Fire Officer in Banking Industry\/ Fire Services with Central\/ State Government.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 30 years and maximum 40 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.42020- 51490\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. ECONOMIST<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 06 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Post Graduate degree in Economics from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
ii) Candidates should have excellent communication and writing skills.
Experience:<\/b> i) Minimum 03 years as an Economist or Analyst with Financial Institutions\/ Banks\/ NBFCs\/ Rating Agencies\/ Analytics Firm\/ Reputed Brokerage Agencies.
ii) Preference will be given to candidates having hands on experience in econometric packages\/ other data sourcing tools like Reuters, Cogenesis.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 24 years and maximum 35 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.42020- 51490\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. SECURITY OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 19 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> A graduate in any discipline from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 05 years experience as an officer in Indian Army not below the rank of Captain and its equivalent rank in Indian Navy, Indian Air Force with minimum 05 years of Commissioned services.
OR
An officer of equivalent rank and service in Police and Central Para Military forces\/ Central Police Organizations.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 26 years and maximum 40 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.31705- 45950\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

4. INTEGRATED TREASURY OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 15 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Graduate in any discipline and MBA\/ PGDM with specialization in Finance\/ Accounting\/ International Business\/ Trade Finance from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
OR
CA\/ ICWA\/ CFA\/ FRM from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
OR
MA (Economics)\/ MS (Economics)\/ MFC from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 23 years and maximum 32 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.23700- 42020\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

5. CREDIT OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 122 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Graduate in any discipline and MBA\/ PGDBA\/ PGDBM\/ PGPM\/ PGDM with specialization in Finance from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
OR
CA\/ ICWA\/ CFA\/ FRM from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
ii) Professional qualification like Financial Risk Management\/ Professional Risk Management will be preferred.
iii) Candidates having specialization in Econometric will be preferred.
iv) Certificate in MS Access, SQL from Globally known organizations such as Microsoft, Cisco etc. or nationally known institute such as NIIT,CMS,APTECH etc. or institutions recognized by the Govt.\/ Govt. regulatory bodies will be preferred.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 23 years and maximum 32 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.23700- 42020\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

6. FOREX OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 18 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Graduate in any discipline from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
AND
MBA\/ PGDBA\/ PGDBM\/ PGPM\/ PGDM with Specialization in Finance\/ International Business\/ Trade Finance from a recognized University\/ Institution\/ Board.
ii) Certificate Course in Forex conducted by IIBF will be preferred.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 23 years and maximum 32 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.23700- 42020\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Note: <\/b>In case of dual specializations, one of the fields of specialization should be in the field prescribed. In case of major\/ minor specializations, major specialization should be in the stream prescribed. Candidates having PG Degree (MMS or MBA)\/ PG Diploma with more than two specializations are not eligible to apply.<\/p>\n

The selected candidates will be on Probation for a period of two years.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any \u2014 As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates\u2019 Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Online Examination\/ Group Discussion <\/b>and\/ or Personal Interview<\/b>. The Online Examination will be tentatively held on 17\/05\/2019.<\/b> The examination will be held at the following centers- Delhi NCR, Chandigarh, Lucknow, Kolkata, Patna, Bhubaneshwar, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Bhopal, Mumbai\/ Navi Mumbai\/ Greater Mumbai\/ Thane and Ahmedabad.<\/i><\/b><\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Union Bank of India (UBI) \u2014 www.unionbankofindia.co.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates\u2019 selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.600\/- (Rs.100\/- for SC\/ST\/PWD candidates)<\/b> as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n
