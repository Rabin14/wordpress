West Bengal PSC-Various Jobs through Miscellaneous Services Recruitment Exam<\/b><\/span><\/div>\n

<\/b><\/span><\/div>\n
Public Service Commission West Bengal is inviting application from the eligible candidates for the <\/span>Miscellaneous Services Recruitment Examination, 2019<\/strong>. On the basis of this examination the qualified candidates will be selected for various posts mentioned below under Government of West Bengal. The candidates will be appointed initially on Temporary basis. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Public Service Commission West Bengal (See URL below). Advertisement No. 13\/2019. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker<\/span><\/div>\n
***************************************************************************************************<\/span><\/div>\n

<\/ins><\/p>\n


<\/span><\/div>\n

<\/span><\/div>\n
Educational Qualification:<\/span><\/strong><\/div>\n
i) A degree from a recognized University or its equivalent.
ii) Ability to read, write and speak in Bengali (not required for those candidates whose mother tongue is Nepali).<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Age Limit:<\/span><\/strong> <\/span><\/div>\n
Minimum 20 years and maximum 39 years as on 01\/01\/2019.<\/span><\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Pay Scale:<\/span><\/strong> <\/span><\/div>\n
Rs.7100-37600\/- with Grade Pay Rs.3900\/- (for the posts at Sl. No. 1 to 11) and Rs.7100-37600\/- with Grade Pay Rs.3600\/- (for the posts at Sl. No. 12 to 19).<\/span><\/div>\n

<\/span><\/div>\n
Candidates\u2019 Selection:<\/strong> <\/span><\/div>\n
Selection of candidates will be through <\/span>Preliminary Examination, Final Examination and Personality Test<\/strong>. The Preliminary Examination will be held at various centres in <\/span>West Bengal<\/strong> tentatively in the month of <\/span>September, 2019<\/strong>. The Final Examination will be held in <\/span>Kolkata<\/strong> and Personality Test will be held thereafter in the office of the Public Service Commission, West Bengal, in Kolkata. For more details please see the Official Advt.<\/span><\/div>\n
Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Public Service Commission West Bengal \u2014 www.pscwbapplication.in<\/u><\/strong><\/span><\/div>\n
Important Dates:<\/span><\/strong><\/div>\n
Starting Date of Online Application: 10\/06\/2019<\/strong><\/div>\n
