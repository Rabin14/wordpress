Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Limited (RCFL) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 83 posts of Officer (Marketing\/ Fire) and Engineer (Electrical\/ Chemical\/ Environmental)<\/b>. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Limited (RCFL) (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below \u2014<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

RASHTRIYA CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS LIMITED (RCFL) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 83 POSTS OF OFFICER (MARKETING\/ FIRE) AND ENGINEER (ELECTRICAL\/ CHEMICAL\/ ENVIRONMENTAL). LAST DATE \u2014 08\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. OFFICER (MARKETING)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 15 Nos. (UR-08, OBC-03, SC-02, ST-01, EWS-01. Out of which, 02 posts are reserved for PWD candidates)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Full time recognized Science\/ Engineering\/ Agriculture Graduation of minimum 03 years with aggregate 60% marks (55% for SC\/ST candidates) and full time recognized Post Graduation degree of 02 years duration with aggregate 60% marks (55% for SC\/ST candidates) in any one of the following-
i) MBA (Marketing as specialization or Major) or,
ii) MMS (Marketing as specialization or Major) or,
iii) MBA (Agri. Business Management).
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 02 years of post qualification sales and marketing experience preferably in agro related industry.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 01\/02\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.40000- 140000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. OFFICER (FIRE)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 05 Nos. (UR-04, OBC-01)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Regular full time BE\/ B.Tech in Fire with 55% marks OR BE\/ B.Tech in Fire and Safety with 55% marks from recognized University\/ Institution.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 02 years post qualification experience.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 01\/02\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.40000- 140000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. ENGINEER (ELECTRICAL)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 20 Nos. (UR-11, OBC-04, SC-03, ST-01, EWS-01. Out of which, 01 post is reserved for PWD candidate)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Regular full time BE\/ B.Tech\/ B.Sc Engineering (Electrical) with 55% aggregate marks (50% aggregate marks for SC\/ST candidates) from recognized University\/ Institution.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 02 years post qualification experience in Electrical Engineering preferably in Fertilizer\/ Chemical\/ Petro Chemical\/ power plant industry.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 01\/02\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.40000- 140000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

4. ENGINEER (CHEMICAL)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 41 Nos. (UR-18, OBC-10, SC-06, ST-03, EWS-04. Out of which, 02 posts are reserved for PWD candidates)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Regular full time BE\/ B.Tech\/ B.Sc Engineering (Chemical) with 55% aggregate marks (50% aggregate marks for SC\/ST candidates) from recognized University\/ Institution.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 02 years post qualification relevant experience in Process industry preferably in Fertilizer\/ Chemical\/ Refinery\/ Petro Chemical industry.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 01\/02\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.40000- 140000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

5. ENGINEER (ENVIRONMENTAL)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 02 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Regular full time BE\/ B.Tech\/ B.Sc Engineering (Environmental Engineering) with 55% aggregate marks from recognized University\/ Institution.
OR
Regular full time BE\/ B.Tech\/ B.Sc Engineering preferably in Chemical discipline with 55% aggregate marks and 02 years PG Degree or Diploma in Environmental Engineering with 55% aggregate marks from recognized University\/ Institution.
OR
Full time Graduation and full time ME\/ M.Tech (Environmental) with 55% aggregate marks from recognized University\/ Institution.
Experience:<\/b> Should have 02 years post qualification experience.
Preference will be given to candidates who have done any distinguish work in Environmental Management.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 32 years as on 01\/02\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.40000- 140000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any \u2014 As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n
