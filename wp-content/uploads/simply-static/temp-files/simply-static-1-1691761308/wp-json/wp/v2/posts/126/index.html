Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 225 posts of District Youth Coordinator, Accounts Clerk cum Typist and Multi Tasking Staff (MTS)<\/b>. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below \u2014<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

NEHRU YUVA KENDRA SANGATHAN (NYKS) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 225 POSTS OF DISTRICT YOUTH COORDINATOR, ACCOUNTS CLERK CUM TYPIST AND MULTI TASKING STAFF (MTS). LAST DATE \u2014 31.03.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. DISTRICT YOUTH COORDINATOR<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 100 Nos. (UR-40, SC-15, ST-08, OBC-27, EWS-10. Out of which, 04 posts are reserved for PWD)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Post Graduate Degree in any discipline from a recognized University or equivalent.
Desirable:<\/b> Three years experience in Youth activities\/ Rural Development\/ other social sectors including voluntary work in a reputed organization.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 28 years as on 01\/01\/2019. as on 31\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.56100-177500\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. ACCOUNTS CLERK CUM TYPIST<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 73 Nos. (UR-30, SC-11, ST-05, OBC-20, EWS-07. Out of which, 03 posts are reserved for PWD & 07 posts for ESM)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) B.Com of a recognized University.
OR
Graduate Degree of a recognized University or equivalent with 02 year experience in Accounts work.
iii) Typing speed of 30 w.p.m. in English or 25 w.p.m. in Hindi.
iv) Knowledge of Computer application.
Desirable:<\/b> i) Experience of two years as National Service Volunteer (NSV)\/ Rashtriya Sadbhavana Yojana (RSY) Volunteer or National Youth Corps (NYC) Volunteer.
ii) PG Degree of Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD) in various courses will be an added advantage.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 28 years as on 31\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.25500-81100\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. MULTI TASKING STAFF (MTS)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 52 Nos. (UR-21, SC-08, ST-04, OBC-14, EWS-05. Out of which, 02 posts are reserved for PWD & 05 posts for ESM)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Matriculation or equivalent.
Desirable:<\/b> i) Worked as NSV\/ RSY\/ NYC for a minimum period of one year.
ii) Training in Basic and Refresher course in Home Guard and Civil defense.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 31\/03\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.18000-56900\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The selected candidates will undergo two year Training cum Probation.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any \u2014 As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates\u2019 Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Online Written Examination, Typing Skill Test<\/b> and Personal Interview<\/b>wherever applicable. The examination will be conducted at various centres across India. For more details please see the Official Advt.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) \u2014 http:\/\/nyks.nic.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates\u2019 selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Examination Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs. 700\/- (Rs. 350\/- for UR\/ EWS\/ OBC Female candidates)<\/b> as Examination Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

NO EXAMINATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC\/ST\/PWD\/ESM CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n
