Cantonment Board Bakloh is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 17 posts of Typist-cum-Record Keeper, Tax Collector, Forest Guard, MHS, Safaiwala<\/b> and JBT Teacher<\/b>. The candidates will be appointed on Direct Recruitment basis. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Cantonment Board Bakloh (See URL below). Advt No. 01\/2019. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

CANTONMENT BOARD BAKLOH INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 17 POSTS OF TYPIST-CUM-RECORD KEEPER, TAX COLLECTOR, FOREST GUARD, MHS, SAFAIWALA AND JBT TEACHER. LAST DATE — 11.05.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. TYPIST-CUM-RECORD KEEPER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Must have passed 10+2 examination or its equivalent from a recognized Board or University.
ii) Must possess a minimum speed of 30 wpm in English typewriting or 25 wpm in Hindi typewriting on Computer.
iii) Must have the knowledge of MS word and Excel on Computer.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 11\/05\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.5910- 20200\/- Grade Pay Rs.1900\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. TAX COLLECTOR<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (SC)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Must have passed 10+2 examination or its equivalent from a recognized Board or University.
ii) Must possess a minimum speed of 30 wpm in English typewriting or 25 wpm in Hindi typewriting on Computer.
iii) Must have the knowledge of MS word and Excel on Computer.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 11\/05\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.5910- 20200\/- Grade Pay Rs.1900\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. FOREST GUARD<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Must have passed 10+2 examination or its equivalent from a recognized Board or University.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 11\/05\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.5910- 20200\/- Grade Pay Rs.2000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

4. MHS<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Must have passed 10+2 examination or its equivalent from a recognized Board or University.
ii) Diploma in Sanitary Inspector Course from a recognized Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 11\/05\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.10300- 34800\/- Grade Pay Rs.4200\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

5. SAFAIWALA<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 10 Nos. (UR-05, OBC-04, EWS-01)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Middle (8th) standard pass.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 11\/05\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.4900- 10680\/- Grade Pay Rs.1300\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

6. JBT TEACHER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 03 Nos. (UR-02, SC-01)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with at least 50% marks and 02 years Junior Basic Teacher (JBT)\/ Diploma in Elementary Education (D.EI.Ed.) (by whatever name known) from an Institute affiliated to any state Board of School Education in accordance with the NCTE(Recognition Norms and Procedure) Regualtions, 2002.
OR
Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with at least 50% marks and 04 years Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.EI.Ed.).
OR
Graduation with at least 50% of marks and Bachelor of Education (B.Ed).
OR
Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with at least 50% marks and 02 years Diploma in Education (Special Education).
(ii) Pass in the Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) or pass in the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET).
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 25 years as on 11\/05\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.5910- 20200\/- Grade Pay Rs.3000\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The candidates will be appointed on Direct Recruitment basis.<\/p>\n

The selected candidates will be on Probation for two years.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Physical Standards (For Forest Guard only): <\/b>
(i) Male Candidates: Height<\/u> — 165 cm; Chest (unexpanded)<\/u> — 79 cm; Chest (expanded)<\/u> — 84 cm
(ii) Female Candidates: Height<\/u> — 150 cm; Chest<\/u>— 74 cm; Chest (expanded)<\/u> — 89 cm<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Written Examination, Skill Test and Physical Efficiency Test (PET),<\/b> wherever applicable.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Cantonment Board Bakloh — www.canttboardrecruit.org<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.500\/-<\/b>as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

NO APPLICATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC\/ST\/PWD\/FEMALE CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of Cantonment Board Bakloh — www.canttboardrecruit.org<\/b><\/u> (See Application form’s link given below) from dated 18.03.2019<\/b>to 11.05.2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful payment of prescribed fee and submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen.
