Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is recruiting eligible candidates for 74 posts of Supervisor (Hospitality)<\/b>. The candidates will be appointed on Contract basis for a period of Two (02) years and extendable by One (01) more year based on requirement and satisfactory performance of the candidates. Interested and eligible candidates may appear directly for the Walk-in-Interview along with prescribed application\/testimonials on below mentioned scheduled dates and venue. No.2019\/IRCTC\/HRD\/SZ\/Rectt.\/ Supervisor (Hospitality). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

INDIAN RAILWAY CATERING AND TOURISM CORPORATION (IRCTC) WILL CONDUCT WALK-IN-INTERVIEW FOR 74 POSTS OF SUPERVISOR (HOSPITALITY). WALK-IN DATE — From 09\/04\/2019 to 12\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. SUPERVISOR (HOSPITALITY)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 74 Nos.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b><\/p>\n

i) Full time B.Sc. in Hospitality & Hotel Administration from recognised Institution.
ii) Good communication skills both verbal and written in English & Hindi.
iii) Possessing knowledge of local language like Tamil, Kannada & Malayalam will be added advantage.<\/p>\n

Experience:<\/b> Minimum 02 years of experience.<\/p>\n

Desirable:<\/b> Knowledge of Computer (MS Offrce) and preparation of reports is desirable.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 01\/03\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.25,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The candidates will be appointed on Contract basis for a period of Two (02) years and extendable by One (01) more year based on requirement and satisfactory performance of the candidates.<\/p>\n

The selected candidate may be posted in Tamil Nadu, Kerala<\/b> and Karnataka<\/b> states.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Walk-in Interview<\/b>.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) – http:\/\/irctc.com<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Date: <\/b>From 09\/04\/2019 to 12\/04\/2019<\/u><\/b> <\/b>from 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM.<\/p>\n

Venue (candidate can appear at any one of the followings places):<\/b>
i)<\/b> Institute of Hotel Management, Catering Technology and Applied Nutrition (Catering College), G.V. Raja Road, Kovalam, Thiruvanthapuram, Kerala- 695527.
