State Bank of India (SBI) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 2000 posts of Probationary Officer (PO)<\/b>. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the State Bank of India (SBI) (See URL below). Advertisement No: CRPD\/ PO\/ 2019-20\/ 01. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

STATE BANK OF INDIA (SBI) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 2000 POSTS OF PROBATIONARY OFFICER (PO). LAST DATE — 22.04.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

PROBATIONARY OFFICER (PO)<\/b><\/p>\n

No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 2000 Nos. (GEN-810, SC-300, ST-150, OBC-540, EWS-200. Out of which, 133 posts are reserved for PWD)<\/i><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b><\/p>\n

i) Graduation in any discipline from a recognised University or any equivalent qualification.
ii) Candidates who are in the Final Year\/ Semester of their Graduation may also apply.
iii) Candidates possessing qualification of Chartered Accountant may also apply.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 21 years and maximum 30 years as on 01\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.23700- 42020\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The selected candidates will be on Probation for two years.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Preliminary Examination, Main Examination<\/b> and Group Exercise & Interview<\/b>. The Preliminary Examination will be held tentatively on 8th, 9th, 15th & 16th June 2019<\/b>. The Main Examination will be held tentatively on 20\/07\/2019.<\/b> Group Exercises & Interview will be held tentatively in the month of September 2019. The examinations will be conducted online in various centres across India.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of State Bank of India (SBI) — https:\/\/www.sbi.co.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.750\/- (Rs.125\/- for SC\/ ST\/ PWD candidates)<\/b> as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of State Bank of India (SBI) — https:\/\/www.sbi.co.in<\/b><\/u> (See Application form’s link given below) from dated 02.04.2019<\/b> to 22.04.2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful payment of prescribed fee and submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen. Candidates must print-out it for future correspondence. DO NOT SEND ANY PRINT-OUT\/HARD COPY OR DOCUMENTS TO ANYWHERE AT THIS STAGE.<\/b> All the verification will be done in due course.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For detailed instruction on how to apply online, please go through the officially released advertisement (See below given link\/ PDF file for more details)<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Important Dates:<\/b><\/p>\n

Starting Date of Online Application: 02.04.2019<\/b><\/p>\n

Closing Date of Online Application: 22.04.2019<\/b><\/p>\n
