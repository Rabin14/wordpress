District Legal Services Authority (DLSA), Nadia is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 10 posts of Para Legal Volunteer (PLV)<\/b>. The vacancies are for Nadia district. Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF below) along with testimonials. Notification No.1\/19\/PLV. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below \u2014<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

DISTRICT LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY (DLSA), NADIA IS INVITING APPLICATION FOR 10 POSTS OF PARA LEGAL VOLUNTEER (PLV). LAST DATE \u2014 18\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

PARA LEGAL VOLUNTEER (PLV)<\/b><\/p>\n

No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 10 Nos.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b> Candidate should be literate, preferably Madhyamik or its equivalent.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years as on 01\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 500\/- per day.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The vacancies are for Nadia district. Only candidates who are local residents of Nadia district are eligible to apply.<\/p>\n

Note:<\/b> Preference will be given to candidates who has residence\/ accommodation in the territorial jurisdiction of Kalyani Court, Ranaghat Court, Tehatta Court and Krishnagar Court<\/b>. For more details please see the Official Advt.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates\u2019 Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Interview\/ Personality Test<\/b>.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of District Legal Services Authority (DLSA), Nadia \u2014 http:\/\/dlsanadia.org<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates\u2019 selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF file below)<\/i> along with attested copies of testimonials in support of Educational Qualification, Caste, Age, Experience certificate (if applicable) and any other relevant documents<\/p>\n

Candidates must also enclose 01 Self-Addressed envelope with the Application Form affixed with postage stamp of Rs.5\/-.<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Last Date and Address:<\/b> The duly filled Application form along with enclosures should reach at the following address \u2014 Office of the District Legal Services Authority, Nadia, ADR Centre Building, Nadia, Krishnagar Judges\u2019 Court Compound, Krishnagar, Nadia, Pin- 741101<\/b>, on or before 18\/04\/2019 upto 05:00 PM.<\/b><\/p>\n

Above given information are in brief. Before applying for the vacancies, please go through the officially released Advertisement<\/p>\n
