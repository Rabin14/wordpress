Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 06 posts of Assistant Tourist Officer<\/b>. The vacancies are in the Directorate of Tourism under the Tourism Department, Govt. of West Bengal. The posts are Temporary but likely to be Permanent. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) (See URL below). Advertisement No. 7\/2019. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION, WEST BENGAL (PSCWB) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 06 POSTS OF ASSISTANT TOURIST OFFICER. LAST DATE — 02\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

ASSISTANT TOURIST OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 06 Nos. (UR-02, OBC-01, SC-02, PWD-01)<\/i><\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Bachelor’s Degree in Travel\/ Tourism Management\/ Tourism Administration from a recognized University.
OR
Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized University with Post-Graduate Diploma in Travel\/ Tourism Management\/ Tourism Administration.
ii) Ability to Read, Write and Speak in Bengali (not required for those candidates whose mother tongue is Nepali).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 39 years as on 01\/01\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs. 7,100\/- – Rs. 37,600\/- with Grade Pay Rs. 3,900\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The vacancies are in the Directorate of Tourism under the Tourism Department, Govt. of West Bengal. The posts are Temporary but likely to be Permanent.<\/p>\n

Reserved Category candidates from the States other than West Bengal shall be treated as General Category candidates.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST and 03 years for OBC category. PWD candidates will get Age relaxation up to 45 years of age.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Screening Test\/ Written Test<\/b> andInterview.<\/b><\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) — http:\/\/www.pscwbapplication.in<\/u><\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs. 160\/-<\/b>as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking OR can be paid through Bank by means of fee payment Challan. Fee Challan can be downloaded from the official website of Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB). See released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

NO APPLICATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC\/ST CANDIDATES OF WEST BENGAL AND PWD CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n
