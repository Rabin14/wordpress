IDBI Bank is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 800 posts of Assistant Managers<\/b>and Executives<\/b>. The candidates will be appointed on Regular\/ Contractual basis. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the IDBI Bank (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

IDBI BANK INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 800 POSTS OF ASSISTANT MANAGERS AND EXECUTIVES. LAST DATE — 15.04.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. ASSISTANT MANAGER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 500 Nos. (UR-228, SC-75, ST-37, OBC-135, EWS-25. Out of which, 15 posts are reserved for PWD)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) A Graduate from a recognized University with minimum 60% marks (55% for SC\/ST\/PWD).
ii) Candidates should have proficiency in Computers.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 21 years and maximum 28 years as on 01\/03\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. EXECUTIVE (ON CONTRACT BASIS)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 300 Nos. (UR-137, SC-45, ST-22, OBC-81, EWS-15. Out of which, 09 posts are reserved for PWD)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) A Graduate from a recognized University with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC\/ST\/PWD).
ii) Candidates should have proficiency in Computers.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 20 years and maximum 25 years as on 01\/03\/2019.
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.22,000\/- for 1st year, Rs.24,000\/- for 2nd year & Rs.27,000\/- for 3rd year per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The candidates will be appointed on Regular\/ Contractual basis.<\/p>\n

Candidates selected for the posts of Executive will be appointed on Contractual basis initially for a period of One year and may be extended on year-to-year basis for a further period of Two years, subject to satisfactory performance of the candidates.<\/p>\n

Candidates who are in the final year of their Graduation\/ Semester may also apply.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Online Test, Personal Interview, Document Verification and Medical Test<\/b>wherever applicable. The Online Test will be held tentatively on 16\/05\/2019<\/b> and 17\/05\/2019.<\/b> The online test will be held at various centres across India. For more details please see the Official Advt.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of IDBI Bank — www.idbi.com<\/u><\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.700\/- (Rs.150\/- for SC\/ST\/PWD candidates)<\/b> as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of IDBI Bank — www.idbi.com<\/u><\/b> (See Application form’s link given below) on or before 15.04.2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful payment of prescribed fee and submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen. Candidates must print-out it for future correspondence. DO NOT SEND ANY PRINT-OUT\/HARD COPY OR DOCUMENTS TO ANYWHERE AT THIS STAGE.<\/b> All the verification will be done in due course.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For detailed instruction on how to apply online, please go through the officially released advertisement (See below given link\/ PDF file for more details)<\/p>\n
