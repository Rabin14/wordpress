Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute (CNCI) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 15 posts of Internal Audit Officer, Technical Officer (Store & Purchase), Assistant Accounts Officer, Social Welfare Officer, Pharmacist<\/b> andUpper Division Clerk<\/b>. The candidates will be appointed on Direct Recruitment basis or on Deputation\/ Absorbtion basis. Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF below) along with testimonials. Advt. No.: 577 \/ 2019. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

CHITTARANJAN NATIONAL CANCER INSTITUTE (CNCI) IS INVITING APPLICATION FOR 15 POSTS OF INTERNAL AUDIT OFFICER, TECHNICAL OFFICER (STORE & PURCHASE), ASSISTANT ACCOUNTS OFFICER, SOCIAL WELFARE OFFICER, PHARMACIST AND UPPER DIVISION CLERK. LAST DATE — 02\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. INTERNAL AUDIT OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Accounts\/ Audit Officer in organized Audit & Accounts Dept. of Govt with at least 05 years’ service in the grade and having experience in Statutory Audit\/ Internal Audit.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 56 years as on 02\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. TECHNICAL OFFICER (STORE & PURCHASE)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Should be a Graduate.
Experience:<\/b> Five years’ experience.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years as on 02\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. ASSISTANT ACCOUNTS OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 01 Nos. (OBC)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Should be a Graduate.
ii) Passed SAS examination or CA.
Experience:<\/b> i) Three years’ experience or over five years’ experience.
ii) Must be able to maintain Accounts independently according to Govt. rules.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years as on 02\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

4. SOCIAL WELFARE OFFICER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 02 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Bachelor’s Degree with two years Diploma in Social Work or Master’s Degree in Social Work.
Desirable:<\/b> Two years’ experience in a reputed Hospital\/ Institution.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 02\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

5. PHARMACIST<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 02 Nos. (UR)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Degree or Diploma in Pharmacy from a recognised Institution\/ University.
ii) Should be registered with the Pharmacy Council.
Desirable:<\/b> Two years’ experience in a reputed Hospital\/ Institution.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 02\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

6. UPPER DIVISION CLERK<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 08 Nos. (UR-06, SC-01,ST-01)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Should be a Graduate.
Experience:<\/b> Three years’ experience. For more details see the Official Advt.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 56 years as on 02\/04\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The candidates will be appointed on Direct Recruitment basis or on Deputation\/ Absorbtion basis. The posts of Internal Audit Officer and Upper Division Clerk will be filled on Deputation\/ Absorbtion basis (See official advertisement for details)<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST and 03 years for OBC category. Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Trade Test<\/b> or Interview<\/b> as applicable.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute (CNCI) — www.cnci.org.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.200\/-<\/b>as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Demand Draft drawn in favour of Director, Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute<\/b>, payable at Kolkata<\/b>.<\/p>\n

NO APPLICATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC, ST, PH & FEMALE CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply on prescribed application format (See PDF file below)<\/i> along with attested copies of testimonials in support of Educational Qualification, Caste, Age, Experience certificate (if applicable) and any other relevant documents<\/p>\n

The envelope containing the print-out\/enclosures should be superscribed on top with — “APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF _____”<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Last Date and Address:<\/b> The duly filled Application form along with enclosures should reach at the following address — The Director, Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute, 37 S.P. Mukherjee Road, Kolkata – 700 026<\/b>, on or before 02\/04\/2019 upto 05:00 PM.<\/b><\/p>\n

Applications may be sent by Ordinary Post or may be dropped in the Application Box in Admn. Block (Research wing) of the Institute.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n
