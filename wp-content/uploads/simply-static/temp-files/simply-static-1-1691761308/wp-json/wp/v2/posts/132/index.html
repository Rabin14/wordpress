Odisha Postal Circle, India Post is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 4392 posts of Gramin Dak Sevaks <\/b>against Gramin Dak Sevaks Cycle-II\/2019. The vacancies are in various divisions under Odisha Postal Circle. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Odisha Postal Circle, India Post (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below \u2014<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

ODISHA POSTAL CIRCLE, INDIA POST INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 4392 POSTS OF BRANCH POST MASTER (BPM), ASSISTANT BRANCH POST MASTER (ABPM) AND DAK SEVAK. LAST DATE \u2014 15.04.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

TOTAL VACANCIES:<\/b><\/u> 4392 posts (UR- 1751, SC- 652, ST- 964, OBC- 474, EWS- 417, PH-134)<\/p>\n

NAME OF THE POSTS AND PAY SCALE:<\/b><\/u>
Gramin Dak Sevak<\/b><\/p>\n

Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.10,000-Rs.24,470\/- or Rs.12,000- Rs.29380\/-<\/p>\n

EDUCATIONAL QUALIFICATION:<\/b>
i) Secondary School Examination pass certificate of 10th standard with passing marks in Mathematics and English (having been studied as compulsory or elective subjects) from any recognized Board.
ii) The candidate should have studied the local language at least up to 10th standard (as compulsory or elective subjects).
iii) Basic Computer Training Course Certificate of at least 60 days duration from any recognized Computer Training Institute.
The requirement of basic computer knowledge certificate shall be relaxable in cases where a candidate has studied computer as a subject in Matriculation or Class XII or any other higher educational level.
iv) Should have knowledge of Cycling. In case of a candidate having knowledge of riding a scooter or motor cycle, that may be considered as knowledge of cycling.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 18 years and maximum 40 years as on 15\/03\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The vacancies are in various divisions under Odisha Postal Circel.<\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Others, if any \u2014 As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates\u2019 Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be on the basis of marks obtained in Academic Qualification.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Odisha Postal Circel, India Post \u2014 https:\/\/www.indiapost.gov.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates\u2019 selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs. 100\/-<\/b>as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking OR can be paid through Post Office. See released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

NO APPLICATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC\/ST, PWD AND FEMALE CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n
