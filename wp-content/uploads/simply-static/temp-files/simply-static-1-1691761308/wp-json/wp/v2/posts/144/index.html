Bank of Baroda (BOB) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 100 posts of Senior Relationship Manager and Territory Head<\/b>. The candidates will be appointed on Contract basis for Wealth Management Services to HNI\/ UHNIs in Bank of Baroda. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Bank of Baroda (BOB) (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

BANK OF BARODA (BOB) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 100 POSTS OF SENIOR RELATIONSHIP MANAGER AND TERRITORY HEAD. LAST DATE — 29.03.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. SENIOR RELATIONSHIP MANAGER<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 96 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Graduate from recognized University or Institution.
ii) Preference will be given to candidates having MBA or equivalent degree from reputed Colleges.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 03 years of experience as a Relationship Manager in Wealth Management with leading Public\/ Private\/ Foreign Banks\/ Asset Management Companies (AMC).
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 25 years and maximum 40 years as on 01\/03\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. TERRITORY HEAD<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 04 Nos.
Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) Graduate from recognized University or Institution.
ii) Preference will be given to candidates having MBA or equivalent degree from reputed Colleges.
Experience:<\/b> Minimum 08 years of experience in Wealth Management out of which minimum 02 years experience as a Team Lead.
Age Limit:<\/b> Minimum 35 years and maximum 45 years as on 01\/03\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Note: <\/b>Preference will be given to candidates having education qualification of MBA or equivalent and possessing IRDA certification and NISM certification for sales and distribution of insurance and mutual fund products respectively.<\/p>\n

The candidates will be appointed on Contract basis for Wealth Management Services to HNI\/ UHNIs in Bank of Baroda.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Shortlisting and\/ or Written Test and\/ or Personal Interview and\/ or Group Discussion.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Bank of Baroda (BOB) — www.bankofbaroda.co.in<\/u><\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Application Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.600\/- (Rs.100\/- for SC\/ ST\/PWD candidates)<\/b> as Application Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of Bank of Baroda (BOB) — www.bankofbaroda.co.in<\/u><\/b> (See Application form’s link given below) from dated 08.03.2019<\/b> to 29.03.2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful payment of prescribed fee and submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen. Candidates must print-out it for future correspondence. DO NOT SEND ANY PRINT-OUT\/HARD COPY OR DOCUMENTS TO ANYWHERE AT THIS STAGE.<\/b> All the verification will be done in due course.<\/p>\n
