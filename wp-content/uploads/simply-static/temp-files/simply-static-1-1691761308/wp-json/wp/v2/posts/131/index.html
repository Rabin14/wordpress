Air India Engineering Services Limited (AIESL) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 80 posts of Graduate Apprentices and Diploma Apprentices<\/b>. The candidates will undergo One year Apprenticeship training under the Apprenticeship (Amendment) Act 1973 in Air India Engineering Services Limited (AIESL). Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Air India Engineering Services Limited (AIESL) (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

AIR INDIA ENGINEERING SERVICES LIMITED (AIESL) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 80 POSTS OF GRADUATE APPRENTICES AND DIPLOMA APPRENTICES. LAST DATE — 25.03.2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. GRADUATE APPRENTICES<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 20 Nos.
Discipline wise vacancies:<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

i) Mechanical: 10 posts
ii) Electrical: 04 posts
iii) Instrumentation: 02 posts
iv) Electronics and Telecommunication: 02 posts
v) Production: 02 posts<\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b> i) A Degree in Engineering or Technology from a recognized University\/ Institution in relevant discipline.
ii) Graduate examination of Professional bodies recognized by the State Government or Central Government as equivalent to above.
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 4984\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. DIPLOMA APPRENTICES<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 60 Nos.
Discipline wise vacancies:<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

i) Mechanical: 25 posts
ii) Electrical: 10 posts
iii) Instrumentation: 10 posts
iv) Electronics and Telecommunication: 10 posts
v) Production: 05 posts<\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b> A Diploma in Engineering or Technology in relevant discipline from a recognized Council\/ Board\/ University\/ Institution or equivalent.
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 3542\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The candidates will undergo One year Apprenticeship training under the Apprenticeship (Amendment) Act 1973 in Air India Engineering Services Limited (AIESL).<\/p>\n

Note<\/b><\/u>:<\/b>
i) Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship under Apprenticeship (Amendment) Act 1973 in any Government or Public Sector or Private industrial organization are not eligible to apply.
ii) Candidate should not have work experience of one year or more.
iii) Candidate should not have completed three years after passing of the qualifying examination.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be on the basis of marks obtained in the Educational Qualification<\/b>.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Air India Engineering Services Limited (AIESL) — www.airindia.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of Air India Engineering Services Limited (AIESL) — www.airindia.in<\/b><\/u> (See Application form’s link given below) from dated 15.03.2019<\/b> to 25.03.2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

NOTE:<\/b> Before applying for the post of Graduate\/ Diploma Apprentices, candidates should first register themselves as Apprentices in the Govt. web portal- www.mhrdnats.gov.in<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen. Candidates must print-out it for future correspondence. DO NOT SEND ANY PRINT-OUT\/HARD COPY OR DOCUMENTS TO ANYWHERE AT THIS STAGE.<\/b> All the verification will be done in due course.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For detailed instruction on how to apply online, please go through the officially released advertisement <\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Important Dates:<\/b><\/p>\n

Starting Date of Online Application: 15.03.2019<\/b><\/p>\n

Closing Date of Online Application: 25.03.2019<\/b><\/p>\n
