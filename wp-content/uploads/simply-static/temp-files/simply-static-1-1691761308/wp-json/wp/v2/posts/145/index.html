2954 ICDS Supervisor under Dept. of WCD&SW, West Bengal – Public Service Commission (PSCWB)<\/b><\/p>\n

Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 2954 posts of Supervisor of ICDS, 2019<\/b>. The vacancies are under the Department of Women & Child Development & Social Welfare, Govt. of West Bengal. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) (See URL below). Advertisement No. 8\/2019. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION, WEST BENGAL (PSCWB) INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 2954 POSTS OF SUPERVISOR OF ICDS, 2019. LAST DATE — 16\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

SUPERVISOR OF ICDS (FEMALE ONLY)<\/b><\/p>\n

No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 2954 Nos. (UR- 1475, SC- 650, ST- 178, OBC- 503, PH- 89, Meritorious Sportsperson- 59)<\/i><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Educational Qualification:<\/b><\/p>\n

i) Graduate in any discipline from a recognized University.<\/p>\n

ii) Ability to Read, Write & Speak in Bengali (not required for those candidates whose mother tongue is Nepali).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 39 years as on 01\/01\/2019.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.7,100\/- to 37,600\/- with Grade Pay Rs. 3,600\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The vacancies are under the Department of Women & Child Development & Social Welfare, Govt. of West Bengal.<\/p>\n

Only Female candidates are eligible to apply for the post. <\/b><\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST and 03 years for OBC category. PWD candidates will get Age relaxation up to 45 years of age. Reserved Category candidates from the States other than West Bengal shall be treated as General Category candidates.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Preliminary Screening Test, Written Examination<\/b> and Viva- Voce Test<\/b>. The Preliminary Screening Test will be held at the following centres- Kolkata, Baruipur, Diamond Harbour, Barrackpore, Barasat, Krishnanagar, Howrah, Chinsurah, Burdwan, Asansol, Purulia, Medinipur, Tamluk, Jhargram, Bankura, Suri, Berhampore, Malda, Balurghat, Raiganj, Jalpaiguri, Alipurduar, Cooch Behar, Siliguri, Kalimpong and Darjeeling<\/b><\/i>. The Written Examination will be held in Kolkata<\/b> only and Viva- Voce Test will be held thereafter in the office of the Public Service Commission, West Bengal, in Kolkata.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) — www.pscwbapplication.in<\/u><\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Examination Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.160\/-<\/b>as Examination Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking OR can be paid through Bank by means of fee payment Challan. Fee Challan can be downloaded from the official website of Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB). See released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

NO EXAMINATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC\/ST CANDIDATES OF WEST BENGAL AND PWD CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSCWB) — www.pscwbapplication.in<\/u><\/b> (See Application form’s link given below) from dated 14\/03\/2019<\/b> to 16\/04\/2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful payment of prescribed fee and submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen. Candidates must print-out it for future correspondence. DO NOT SEND ANY PRINT-OUT\/HARD COPY OR DOCUMENTS TO ANYWHERE AT THIS STAGE.<\/b> All the verification will be done in due course.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For detailed instruction on how to apply online, please go through the officially released advertisement (See below given link\/ PDF file for more details)<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Important Dates:<\/b><\/p>\n
