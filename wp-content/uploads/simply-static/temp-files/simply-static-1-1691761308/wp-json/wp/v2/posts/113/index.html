Indian Navy is inviting application from the eligible candidates for 172 posts of Chargeman (Mechanic\/ Ammunition & Explosive)<\/b>. Indian Navy Civilian Entrance Test (INCET-CM (MECH) & CM (AMMN & EXPL))- 02\/2019. Interested and eligible candidates may apply Online only through the official website of the Indian Navy (See URL below). Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

INDIAN NAVY INVITES ONLINE APPLICATION FOR 172 POSTS OF CHARGEMAN (MECHANIC\/ AMMUNITION & EXPLOSIVE). LAST DATE — 28\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. CHARGEMAN (MECHANIC)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 103 Nos. (UR-41, SC-18, ST-06, OBC-28, EWS-10. Out of which, 09 posts are reserved for PWD)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Diploma in Mechanical or Electrical or Electronics or Production Engineering from a recognized University or Institute.
Experience:<\/b> Two years working experience in quality control or quality assurance or testing or proof in the area of design or production or maintenance of Engineering equipment or system from a recognized organisation.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 28\/04\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.35400- 112400\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. CHARGEMAN (AMMUNITION & EXPLOSIVE)<\/b>
No. of Vacancies<\/u>: 69 Nos. (UR-25, SC-13, ST-07, OBC-18, EWS-06. Out of which, 05 posts are reserved for PWD)<\/i>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Diploma in Chemical Engineering from a recognized University or Institute.
Experience:<\/b> Two years experience in quality control or quality assurance or testing or proof in the area of chemical engineering or processing from recognized organisation.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 30 years as on 28\/04\/2019.
Pay Scale:<\/b> Rs.35400- 112400\/-<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The selected candidates will be posted to various Naval Armament Inspectorate (NAI) Units at Depots\/ Ordnance Factories\/ DRDO Labs\/ Quasi Military Establishments.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST, 03 years for OBC and 10 years for PWD category. Ex-Serviceman & Others, if any — As per Govt. norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Online Computer Based Written Examination and Document Verification.<\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Indian Navy — www.indiannavy.nic.in<\/b><\/u><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For more details regarding candidates’ selection norms and eligibility norms, go through the officially released advertisement (See URL\/PDF below).<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Examination Fee:<\/b> Candidates must pay Rs.205\/-<\/b>as Examination Fee. Fee can be paid through Online mode during submission of online application form by using Debit\/Credit card or Net banking or any other option available. See officially released advertisement for more details.<\/p>\n

NO EXAMINATION FEE REQUIRED IN CASE OF SC\/ST\/PWBD\/EX-SERVICEMEN AND WOMEN CANDIDATES.<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How to Apply:<\/b> Interested and eligible candidates may apply online only through the official website of Indian Navy — www.indiannavy.nic.in<\/b><\/u> (See Application form’s link given below) from dated 16\/04\/2019<\/b> to 28\/04\/2019.<\/b><\/p>\n

Upon successful payment of prescribed fee and submission of online application form, the system generated Registration\/ Acknowledgement Slip with unique registration number will appear on the computer screen. Candidates must print-out it for future correspondence. DO NOT SEND ANY PRINT-OUT\/HARD COPY OR DOCUMENTS TO ANYWHERE AT THIS STAGE.<\/b> All the verification will be done in due course.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

For detailed instruction on how to apply online, please go through the officially released advertisement (See below given link\/ PDF file for more details)<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Important Dates:<\/b><\/p>\n

Starting Date of Online Application: 16\/04\/2019<\/b><\/p>\n

Closing Date of Online Application: 28\/04\/2019<\/b><\/p>\n
