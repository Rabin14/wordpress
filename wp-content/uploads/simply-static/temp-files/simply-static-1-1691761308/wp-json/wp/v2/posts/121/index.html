Directorate of Plant Protection Quarantine & Storage is recruiting eligible candidates for 186 posts of Senior Technical Officer (STO) and Technical Officer (TO)<\/b>. The vacancies are in the Offices of the Directorate of Plant Protection Quarantine & Storage located at various Regions\/ Stations. The candidates will be appointed on Contractual basis from 01\/04\/2019 to 31\/03\/2020. Interested and eligible candidates may appear directly for the Walk-in-Interview along with prescribed application\/testimonials on below mentioned scheduled dates and venue. Educational qualification required and other details, in brief, for informational purpose only in interest of job-seeker, are given below —<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

DIRECTORATE OF PLANT PROTECTION QUARANTINE & STORAGE WILL CONDUCT WALK-IN-INTERVIEW FOR 186 POSTS OF SENIOR TECHNICAL OFFICER (STO) AND TECHNICAL OFFICER (TO). WALK-IN DATE — From 01\/04\/2019 to 05\/04\/2019<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

NAME OF THE POSTS AND TOTAL VACANCIES:<\/u><\/b>
1. Senior Technical Officer (STO): 41 posts
2. Technical Officer (TO): 145 posts<\/p>\n

DETAILS REGARDING QUALIFICATION, AGE, PAY ETC. ARE GIVEN BELOW:<\/u><\/b><\/p>\n

1. SENIOR TECHNICAL OFFICER (STO)- TOXICOLOGY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Doctorate Degree in Veterinary Pharmacology\/ Veterinary Pathology\/ Animal Biotechnology and Veterinary Physiology from recognized University\/ Institution.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years (45 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 53,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. SENIOR TECHNICAL OFFICER (STO)- CHEMISTRY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Doctorate Degree in Agricultural Chemistry or Doctoral Degree in Chemistry from a recognized University\/ Institution.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years (45 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 53,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. SENIOR TECHNICAL OFFICER (STO)- ENTOMOLOGY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Doctorate degree in Entomology or Nematology or Doctorate degree in Agriculture with specialization in Entomology or Nematology of Doctorate degree in Zoology with specialization in Entomology or Nematology from a recognized University or Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years (45 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 53,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

4. SENIOR TECHNICAL OFFICER (STO)- PLANT PATHOLOGY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Doctorate degree in Plant Pathology of Doctorate degree in Agriculture with specialization in Plant Pathology or Doctorate degree in Botany with specialization in Plant Pathology from a recognized University or Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years (45 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 53,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

5. SENIOR TECHNICAL OFFICER (STO)- WEED SCIENCE<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Doctorate degree in Agriculture (Agronomy) or Doctorate degree in Botany with specialization in Weed Science from a recognized University or Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 40 years (45 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs. 53,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

6. TECHNICAL OFFICER (TO)- TOXICOLOGY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> Master’s Degree in Veterinary Science OR Master’s Degree in Laboratory Technology with specialization in Toxicology or Master’s degree in Zoology with specialization in Animal Physiology or Master’s degree in Bio-technology from a recognized University\/ Institution.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 35 years (40 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.37,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

7. TECHNICAL OFFICER (TO)- CHEMISTRY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> M.Sc. Degree in Agricultural Chemistry or Chemistry with specialization in Organic Chemistry or in Analytical Chemistry from a recognized University\/ Institution.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 35 years (40 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.37,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

8. TECHNICAL OFFICER (TO)- ENTOMOLOGY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> M.Sc. Degree in Entomology or Nematology of M.Sc. in Agriculture with specialization in Entomology or Nematology of M.Sc. degree in Zoology with specialization in Entomology or Nematology from a recognized University or Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 35 years (40 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.37,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

9. TECHNICAL OFFICER (TO)- PLANT PATHOLOGY<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> M.Sc. Degree in Plant Pathology of M.Sc. degree in Agriculture with specialization in Plant Pathology or M.Sc. degree in Botany with specialization in Plant Pathology from a recognized University or Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 35 years (40 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.37,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

10. TECHNICAL OFFICER (TO)- WEED SCIENCE<\/b>
Educational Qualification:<\/b> M.Sc. in Agriculture (Agronomy) or M.Sc. degree in Botany with Weed Science as a subject from a recognized University or Institute.
Age Limit:<\/b> Maximum 35 years (40 years for Female\/ Transgender candidates).
Consolidated Pay:<\/b> Rs.37,000\/- per month.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The vacancies are in the Offices of the Directorate of Plant Protection Quarantine & Storage located at various Regions\/ Stations. The candidates will be appointed on Contractual basis from 01\/04\/2019 to 31\/03\/2020.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Upper Age Relaxation: <\/b>Upper age limit is relaxable by 05 years for SC\/ST category.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Candidates’ Selection:<\/b> Selection of candidates will be through Interview.<\/b><\/p>\n

Exact Date, Time and Venue for examination\/interview will be intimated to the eligible candidates in due course as well as such information will be also available in the official website of Directorate of Plant Protection Quarantine & Storage – http:\/\/ppqs.gov.in<\/b><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n
